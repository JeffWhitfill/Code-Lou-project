Explanation of Yin Yang Symbol

The white and black represent the balance of life. The whole is greater than either half
You can think of it as gathering energy vs expending energy. The dark side is thought of as the feminine
side or gathering side where as the light side is the masculine side and expending energy.

The two dots in each figure are to illustrate the presence of the other energy within it.
Energy needs to be gathered before it can be expelled.
